# PoseTracker

PoseTracker includes built-in exercise tracking for movements such as:

- squats
- push-ups
- lunges
- planks

It can automatically detect:

- repetitions
- posture issues
- progression
- exercise duration

PoseTracker can also compare user movements to a **reference exercise** for technique analysis.
